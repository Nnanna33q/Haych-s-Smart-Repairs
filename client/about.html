About Us

About Haych's Smart Repairs

We are a locally owned vehicle body repair business based in Hall Green, B28 9ND, built on a passion for quality workmanship and attention to detail. Founded by Haych, the business focuses on delivering high-standard repairs that restore vehicles to their best possible condition.

We specialise in smart repairs, paint correction, paint touch-ups, and professional detailing, helping drivers keep their vehicles looking sharp without the cost or inconvenience of full body shop repairs. Whether it’s a bumper scuff, minor ding, light scratches, or a full paint refresh, every job is handled with care and precision.

Frequently Asked Questions About Our Services

Smart repair fixes small areas of damage on a vehicle instead of the whole panel. We check the damage, prepare the area, and carry out the repair, such as paint touch-ups, polishing, or dent removal. The work is usually done quickly, and leave the car looking clean and well restored.

We can provide a rough estimate, but a full assessment can only be done once the vehicle has been inspected, either in person or through photos.

No, you don’t need to be present while the repair is carried out. We can complete most SMART repairs quickly and efficiently on-site. You can drop off your car or have it repaired at your location, and it will be ready without you needing to wait.

Most smart repairs don’t require any special aftercare. Your vehicle can be used as normal once the repair is finished. In some cases, we may give a few quick tips like avoiding washing the repaired area for a short period. But generally, no extra steps are needed.
